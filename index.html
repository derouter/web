How DeRouter Works

1

Providers Offer Services

Providers publish digital service offers with protocol specifications and pricing. Current implementation includes OpenAI-compatible services.

2

Network Discovers Offers

DeRouter nodes connect via P2P using mDNS and bootstrap nodes. The network maintains an up-to-date list of available services.

3

Consumers Connect & Pay

Consumers find the best matching offers and connect directly. Future implementations will include cryptocurrency payments.
